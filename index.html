About Me

Hello, I'm a third-year Computer Science student at John Abbott College, based in Montreal, Quebec. I'm enthusiastic about crafting new apps and websites. I built my first website when I was 13 years old with nothing more than a Notepad document, and became hooked with creating different programs or websites.

During my time in John Abbott College I have become experienced with problem-solving, building applications and websites using C#, Java, Linux Bash, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, nodeJS, Kotlin, and Jetpack Compose.

Additionally, I have worked with different tools and frameworks such as Visual Studio, Visual Studio Code, ASP.Net, WPF, GitHub, Jira, Microsoft Office Suite, and Docker.

I'm good with working independently but I am equally comfortable working with a team to achieve a common goal. I am eager to acquire new skills and apply them to further my lifelong passion of learning.

In my spare time I like to read, play video games, walking and exploring my city.

I graduated previously from John Abbott College with a degree in Media Arts (2006), and I graduated with Honours from Dawson College with a degree in 3D Animation and CGI (2012).
